Our Journey

How Event-Simplified came to be

From a small idea to a platform trusted by thousands of event professionals worldwide.

Milestones & Growth

2018

The Idea Sparks

After coordinating one too many chaotic corporate events using spreadsheets and email chains, our founder decided there had to be a better way. Event-Simplified was conceived on a napkin in a Toronto coffee shop.

2019

First Version Launches

After months of development and customer discovery interviews, we launched our first version to a closed beta of 50 event planners. Feedback was overwhelming — people loved it.

2020

Pivoting to Hybrid & Virtual

When the world shifted overnight, we were ready. We rapidly built virtual and hybrid event tools, helping over 500 clients transition their events online without missing a beat.

2021

Series A & Major Expansion

Raised $8M Series A to expand our team and build deeper integrations. Crossed 2,000 active clients and launched our mobile attendee app to widespread acclaim.

2022

AI Features Introduced

We integrated AI-powered scheduling, budget prediction, and post-event analysis — becoming one of the first platforms to bring machine learning to event management.

2024

12,000+ Events & Growing

Today we're proud to power events in over 40 countries, with a team of 80+ people dedicated to making every event experience exceptional.

The all-in-one event management platform for modern teams.
