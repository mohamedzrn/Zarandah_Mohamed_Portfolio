Our Mission

Building technology that puts people first

Event-Simplified was born from a simple belief: organizing great events shouldn't require a PhD in logistics. We've built a platform that takes the chaos out of event management — so you can focus on what matters: delivering incredible experiences.

We work with everyone from solo consultants running intimate workshops to enterprise teams orchestrating conferences for thousands. Whatever your scale, our platform grows with you.

Based in Toronto, our team of 80+ spans engineers, designers, and event professionals who've collectively planned over 5,000 events. We eat our own cooking — Event-Simplified runs every internal event we host.
