Our Foundation

Built on Three Pillars

Foundation Sixty6 was created with a singular purpose: to make mental health support accessible, compassionate, and empowering for everyone.

Empathy

We listen without judgment. Every experience is valid, every feeling is acknowledged. You are heard here.

Education

Understanding is the first step to healing. We provide workshops, articles, and guides to help you learn and grow.

Empowerment

We believe in your strength. Our platform equips you with tools and connections to take charge of your wellbeing.

What We Address

Understanding the Challenges

Mental health challenges are more common than you think. Recognizing them is the first step toward recovery.

01

Anxiety

More than just worry. Anxiety can manifest as persistent dread, physical tension, and overwhelming fear that interferes with daily life. You deserve to feel calm again.

301M People affected globally
63% Go untreated
02

Depression

Not just sadness. Depression is a heavy fog that dulls joy, drains energy, and makes each day feel insurmountable. Recovery is possible, and help is available.

280M People affected globally
75% In low-income untreated
03

Burnout

When exhaustion becomes the norm. Burnout strips away motivation, creates cynicism, and leaves you feeling empty. Boundaries and rest are not luxuries, they are necessities.

77% Of workers affected
2.5x Risk of health issues
