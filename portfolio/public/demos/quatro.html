Our Story

From lunch boxes
to happy hours.

For over a decade, Quatro was the juice brand that fueled recess and road trips. When our original fans grew up, so did we. We traded sugar rushes for craft seltzer and reimagined everything -- same bold spirit, whole new vibe.

2009
The Juice Era

Quatro launches as a bold, four-fruit juice blend beloved by kids and parents alike. The colorful cans become a lunchbox staple.

2019
The Pivot

Research shows our original fans are now in their twenties. We ask: what if we grew up with them? The seltzer project begins.

2021
Quatro Reborn

The first craft seltzer line drops. Same bold flavors, zero sugar, all grown up. The rebrand is instant -- and the cooler is never the same.

Today
Bold by Nature

Four core flavors, a cult following, and a mission: make the most refreshing seltzer on the planet. No shortcuts. No compromise.
