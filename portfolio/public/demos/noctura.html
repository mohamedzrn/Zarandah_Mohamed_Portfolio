Noctura Pulse

An AI-powered Instagram trend bot that turns creator behaviour into content intelligence — silently.

About the Project

Noctura Pulse is a stealth Instagram DM bot built for a creator management agency. Creators forward reels, carousels, stories, and clips — the bot analyses each piece with Claude Vision AI and syncs intelligence to per-creator Notion databases automatically.

Highlights
  • Adaptive DM polling (45s to 1hr backoff) waking within 30s on activity.
  • Supports 5 content types: Reels, Carousels, Static posts, Stories, and xma_clip format.
  • Per-creator Notion database routing with auto-set thumbnail gallery covers.
  • Story media captured before 24-hour expiry.
  • Audio bank and keyword bank ranked by cross-creator frequency and reach.
  • Human-paced reply delays (12–50s randomised) with residential proxy for account safety.
