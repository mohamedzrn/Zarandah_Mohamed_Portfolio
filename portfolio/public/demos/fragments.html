Synopsis
The Story

In the liminal space between memory and imagination, a solitary figure confronts fragmented echoes of a life once lived. As the boundaries of time and perception dissolve, each fragment reveals a deeper truth about the nature of reality itself — and the courage it takes to see it whole.

The Process
From Vision to Screen
01

Pre-Production

Months of conceptual development, storyboarding each fragmented sequence, and scouting locations that embodied the liminal spaces central to the narrative. Every detail was mapped before a single frame was captured.

02

Production

Shot over an intensive period using innovative camera techniques — including in-camera reflections, controlled lens distortion, and choreographed lighting shifts — to capture reality as something perpetually in flux.

03

Post-Production

Assembled in Adobe Premiere and refined with After Effects compositing. Layered VFX work merged practical footage with digital artistry, creating the seamless fragmentation effect that defines the film's visual identity.
