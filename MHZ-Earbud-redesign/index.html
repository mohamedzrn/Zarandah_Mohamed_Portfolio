Features

Engineered for excellence

Every feature crafted to elevate your listening experience.

01

High-Quality Audio

Crystal-clear sound in every frequency

MHZ earbuds feature precision-tuned 10mm dynamic drivers with an extended frequency response of 20Hz–40kHz. Whether you're catching the deepest bass or the most delicate highs, every note is reproduced faithfully.

02

Wireless Connectivity

True wireless. Zero compromise.

Bluetooth 5.3 delivers an instant, rock-solid connection with a range of up to 30 meters. Multi-device pairing means seamlessly switching between your phone, laptop, and tablet — without missing a beat.

03

Comfortable Design

Wear them all day

Ergonomically shaped with memory foam ear tips in three sizes, MHZ earbuds conform to your ear canal for a secure, fatigue-free fit. Weighing just 5g each, you'll forget you're wearing them.

04

Long Battery Life

30 hours of playtime

8 hours per charge plus a 22-hour charging case. With 10-minute quick charge giving you 2 hours of playback, you'll never be left in silence. IPX5 sweat resistance means they move with your life.

05

Touch Controls

Control at your fingertips

Capacitive touch sensors respond to single, double, and triple taps for seamless control of playback, calls, ANC, and voice assistant activation. No fumbling, no buttons — just intuitive gestures.
